Balviora ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what personal data we collect when you visit our website or join our founding circle, why we collect it, and the choices you have.

1. Information we collect

  • Contact details you give us — such as your first name and email address when you join the waitlist or request a product recommendation.
  • Communication preferences and your marketing consent status.
  • Usage data — if you accept analytics, aggregated and pseudonymised information about how you use the site (pages viewed, approximate region, device type).

We do not knowingly collect data from children, and we do not sell your personal data.

2. How we use it

  • To send founding-member updates, educational content, and product availability notices you have opted into (double opt-in).
  • To provide the personalised product recommendation you request.
  • To understand and improve the experience, only where you have consented to analytics.
  • To meet legal, tax, and security obligations.

3. Legal bases (GDPR)

Where GDPR applies, we rely on: your consent (marketing and analytics), legitimate interests (securing and improving our site), and legal obligation (record-keeping). You may withdraw consent at any time.

4. Sharing & processors

We share data only with processors who help us operate, under contract and appropriate safeguards, such as:

  • Email marketing platform — [Klaviyo / Mailchimp / Brevo / ConvertKit]
  • Analytics — [Google Analytics 4 / Microsoft Clarity], only with consent
  • Hosting & infrastructure — [hosting provider]

Where data is transferred outside your region, we use recognised transfer mechanisms such as Standard Contractual Clauses.

5. Cookies & analytics

We use a consent-first approach: non-essential cookies and analytics load only after you accept them in our cookie banner. See our Cookie Policy for details and controls.

6. Your rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, port, or restrict the processing of your data, to object to processing, and to withdraw consent. Under the CCPA/CPRA, California residents may request disclosure or deletion and opt out of any "sale" or "sharing" (we do neither). To exercise any right, contact us below — we will not discriminate against you for doing so.

7. Data retention

We keep your data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law. If you unsubscribe, we remove you from active marketing promptly and retain minimal suppression data to honour your choice.

8. Contact

Questions or requests: privacy@balviora.com. If you are in the EU/UK and are unsatisfied, you may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
