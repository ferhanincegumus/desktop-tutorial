Cookie Policy

Last updated: 19 July 2026

Template notice. This document is a starting template and is not legal advice. Complete the bracketed details and confirm the cookie inventory before launch.

Cookies are small files stored on your device. We take a consent-first approach: only strictly necessary cookies are set by default, and analytics or marketing technologies load only after you accept them in our cookie banner.

1. Categories we use

Strictly necessary (always on)

Required for the site to function — for example, remembering your cookie choice. These do not require consent.

Analytics (consent required)

Help us understand aggregated usage so we can improve the experience — for example, [Google Analytics 4] and [Microsoft Clarity].

Marketing (consent required)

Used to measure campaigns and reach — for example, [Meta Pixel, Pinterest Tag, TikTok Pixel]. None of these load unless you accept.

2. Consent Mode

We implement a consent-mode pattern: tracking storage defaults to "denied" until you accept. You can change your choice at any time by clearing the balviora-consent value in your browser or using the banner when it reappears.

3. Managing cookies

  • Use the banner to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Adjust or block cookies in your browser settings.
  • Opt out of many advertising cookies via industry tools such as Your Online Choices or the NAI.

4. More information

For how we handle the data cookies may collect, see our Privacy Policy. Questions: privacy@balviora.com.
